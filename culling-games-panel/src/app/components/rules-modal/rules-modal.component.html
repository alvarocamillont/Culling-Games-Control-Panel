Culling Game Declarative Rules

The binding vow governing Kenjaku's game board

"Players are given points by killing other sorcerers. Points correspond to the life of Jūjutsu Sorcerers or Non-Sorcerers..."

RULE 1 • DECLARATIVE PARTICIPATION

Upon gaining a cursed technique or waking up inside a barrier, players must declare participation in the Culling Game in their starting colony within 19 days.

RULE 2 • PENALTY OF DEATH

Any player who breaks Rule 1 shall suffer cursed technique extraction, which inevitably results in immediate brain death and cardiac arrest.

RULE 5 • ACQUIRING POINTS

Players obtain points by taking the life of other participants. Shaman/Sorcerer deaths yield 5 points. Non-shaman deaths yield 1 point.

RULE 10 • POINT TRANSFER

Players may transfer any number of their points to another participant. Koganes will act as the intermediary agent for all score adjustments.

RULE 11 • CUSTOM RULE ADDITION

Except where it would have a significant negative impact on the longevity of the game, players may spend 100 points to negotiate and add a new rule to the Game Board with their Kogane.
